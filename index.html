ABOUT US

🏛️ Calicut Cosmopolitan Club

Established in 1898, the Calicut Cosmopolitan Club stands as a symbol of heritage, fellowship, and refined social living in Kerala. Founded by Mr. P. Karunakara Menon, the club was envisioned as a space to foster community spirit and cultural exchange. Today, it continues to thrive as a prestigious hub for social, recreational, and intellectual pursuits, welcoming members from all walks of life.
